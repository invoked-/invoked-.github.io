How it works

Feedback at the time of cancellation is some of the most valuable feedback you'll recieve as a business. Invoked makes it easy to take action, allowing you to shape future product decisions and strategy.

By hooking into an existing subscription cancelation form (or a new one), Invoked simplifies the process of understanding customer churn. It supports both dropdown/radio button feedback and longer explanations from a text box, as well as the display of trends, analytics and individual user feedback.

This is just the start: We're already thinking of other enhancements and features to assist in lowering user churn.
